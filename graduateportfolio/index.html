RHEA BRAITHWAITE PORTFOLIO

committed to building tools and experiences

that make people feel seen

SafeSound

Mobile application that detects AI-manipulated audio in real-time to help protect users from voice-based fraud.

WalkWithYou

Mobile application that provides users with tools to make their traveling journey safer.

Desert Whispers

An interactive installation, centered around a single tree in the Sweihan Abu Dhabi desert, using sensors and LED lights to convey the tree's limits of human interaction.

Culinary Chronicles

A virtual reality installation of a Saudi Arabian village, bringing to life the unseen history of artist Maha Malluh's artwork, "Food for Thought".

Digital Waste

A virtual reality project of a forest scattered with piles of trash and television screens that play videos depicting the harsh realities of the climate crisis.

Switch Notes

Arudino based game where song notes fall down the screen in four different lanes. The user presses the matching switch for each lane to play the note and increase their score.
