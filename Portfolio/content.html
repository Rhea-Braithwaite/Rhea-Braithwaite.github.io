COMMUNICATIONS LAB PORTFOLIO

these works she created not alone,

but with students each with unique assets different from her own.

they put their forces together to face the task,

and here are hers and their works, finished at last.
30MFF - 4 Years of College

Step into "4 Years of College", the first chapter in this journey. It focuses on a 30 minute film festival done in the very first of these arduous lessons. 4 students sweat and toiled to create a moving picture within half an hour. The result was a testimonial of how these 4 learners felt during their years of studying. It has been placed on a site which describes the process and meaning behind the piece.

Forger of the site: Rhea
Comic - Just Moved In

Boo! What’s that? A sad soul trapped in a house all alone. The new owner faces the spirit and you decide how this story ends. “Just Moved In” is a comic filled with panels outlining the experience of a man with a ghost in his newly bought home. The site gives the user choices on how to handle this ghostly spectacle, each with a unique outcome.

Forgers of the Site: Rhea, Moyo, Maimuna, Bhavicka
Sound - Time Traveller

Time Traveler: Technology through the Eras, is our third chapter. We delve into the world of sound, how to create it and shape it. This site provides an auditory experience of the technological innovation of man, but with the reminder of the consequences of the drive to push humanity forward. Take a peek to learn more about technology through the eras but by sound.

Forgers of the Site: Rhea, Moyo, Seif, Yunho
Video - Tutoruu: An Academic Guide

Struggling with classes or assignments? Has the professor provided you with all the scrolls, yet still they offer no help? Then try the app Tutoruu. “Tutoruu: An Academic Guide”, takes you on a visual journey through a student’s struggle to complete an assignment. YOU decide the potentially best solutions, and see those actions come to life on screen. However, they may not be enough and the app Tutoruu is our protagonist's only solution.

Forgers of the Site: Rhea, Moyo, Seif, Sadeq
